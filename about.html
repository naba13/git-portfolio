Dinner In The Sky

Dinner In The Sky is a glamorous fine-dine eatery located on the alluring lawn inside of Pavilion End Club. It’s completely constructed out of canes that add to the festive and tribal aesthetic of the restaurant and provides a stimulating time for any visitor who’s looking to dine-in.

The restaurant provides a vibrant line-up of cultural cuisines that are mouth-watering, tantalizing and also eye-appealing in our a la carte line-up. Whether its birthday parties, corporate events, family dinner or any other occasions; Dinner In The Sky believes in providing first-rate service and dining that’s class apart, making your evening or event have an everlasting impression.

Dinner In The Sky also devises corporate and family events and birthdays for our esteemed clients at compatible prices on Friday, Saturday and Sunday.
